Data Dictionary & Domain Definitions

1. Account Management Domain

Owner: Jane Doe

Definition: A collection of service domains responsible for handling various aspects of banking account operations.

Entity / Element Description Subdomain
Account Container for records associated with a business arrangement for regular transactions and services. Account Core
Balance Amount of money available or owed. Account Core
Account Holder Party that owns an account. Account Parties
Accounting Transaction Event recognized by an entry in the records of an account. Account Transactions
Account Statement Periodic summary of account activity for a given period of time. Account Reporting
Demand Deposit A bank account (like a checking account) from which funds can be withdrawn at any time without advance notice. Deposit Accounts

2. Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Domain

Owner: Jane Doe

Definition: Governs the full lifecycle of customer engagement, including onboarding, relationship development, and eventual closure.

Entity / Element Description Subdomain
Customer A party who receives a service or product from a seller or service provider. Party Management
Beneficial Owner Party that enjoys the benefits of ownership of something even though title may be in another name. Party Ownership
Business Entity An organization formed to conduct business, engage in commerce, or carry out a mission. Legal Entity Management
Address Index to a location to which communications may be delivered. Party Contact Information
Agreement Negotiated understanding between two or more parties reflecting offer and acceptance. Party Agreements
Affiliate Party that is related to a legal entity, directly or indirectly controlling or controlled by it. Party Relationships
